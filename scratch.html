My Trip to Vietnam

This past August, myself and four friends traveled to Vietnam for two weeks. We visited Da Lat, Nha Trang, Hoi An, and Da Nang, Ho Chi Minh City. We experienced the local culture, ate lots of pho and other food, and scenery. We visited the beaches, temples, and night markets. We also went canyoneering, hiking, to an amusement park, and other adventures. We got to visit South Korea on the way back as well during a long layover. It was a lot of fun and convinced us to plan another trip next year.
